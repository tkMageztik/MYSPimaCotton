About us

M&S PIMA COTTON is a family-run business. By the time we embarked on this project, back in 2014, we already had 15 years of experience working in the textile industry.

Having gained formative experience by working at San Cristobal and Nettalco Textile Company, we decided to set up our own project as M&S PIMA COTTON. In the same year we came into commercial operation, we started to export our products to one of the main export markets, United States.
